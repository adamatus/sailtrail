About SailTrail

SailTrail was born out of a passion for windsports and technology and inspired by activity tracking websites for other genres (e.g., Strava and Runkeeper).

SailTrail was built by Adam Riggall, a lifelong sailor and technology-lover. Adam grew up sailing Sunfish and Barnett 1400s on Kelly Lake in Wisconsin. He eventually found his way to the Hoofer Sailing Club at the University of Wisonsin, Madison. With Hoofers he learned to windsurf, snowkite, and race a variety of sailboats (J22s, scows). These days Adam can be found sailing the Columbia River in his Cal 20 "Bessie" and racing with the vibrant Portland, OR sailing community.
